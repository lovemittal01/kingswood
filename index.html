          We've Got Answers!

          Ques: What vehicle is right for my needs?

          Ans: Our team at Kingswood Auto Service is dedicated to helping you find the right vehicle for your needs.
          We consider factors such as your lifestyle, preferences, and budget to guide you toward a choice that meets your requirements.
          Feel free to ask our knowledgeable staff for assistance in selecting the perfect vehicle for you.

          Ques: How do I know I'm getting the best deal?

          Ans: We guarantee the best deal by offering transparent pricing, flexible terms, and competitive interest rates
          the lowest guaranteed! Our commitment to customer satisfaction is reflected in our positive reviews, ensuring you
          get not only a great deal but also an exceptional car-buying experience.

          Ques: How much do I have to make each month for approval?

          Ans: To meet our financing approval standards, a monthly income of $1800+ is required.
          This ensures a straightforward process and increases your chances of approval.
          If your income doesn't reach the requirement, you can consider adding someone onto your application.

          Ques: How will applying for financing
          affect my credit score?

          Ans: Applying for financing at Kingswood Auto Service involves a standard credit inquiry.
          Rest assured, we take measures to minimize any impact on your credit score. Once you get your
          new vehicle, you will be building up your credit with your new recreation, leisure, or auto loan!
